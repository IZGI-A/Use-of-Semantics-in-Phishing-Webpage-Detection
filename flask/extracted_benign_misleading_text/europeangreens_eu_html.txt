We are thrilled to welcome you to our 30th European Green Party Council in Finland’s third biggest city Tampere between 8-10 November. It will be a unique opportunity to meet Green leaders from across Europe in one of Finland’s Greenest municipalities, share best practices and chart a way forward as we stand on the cusp of a new decade.
The green local councillors conference “Cities for the Future - The Green Way” took place in in Oslo, the European Green Capital 2019, on 21-22 June.
