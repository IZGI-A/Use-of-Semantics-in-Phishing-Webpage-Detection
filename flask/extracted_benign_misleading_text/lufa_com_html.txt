A customizable weekly food basket
Once you’re subscribed, we automatically build a base basket for you every week with $30-worth of our veggies. Customize it by adding or removing whatever you want - minimum order is only $20. Your basket is weekly by default, but you can suspend it with one click in your account settings, or suspend deliveries for longer if you’re travelling. And you can cancel anytime too by giving us a quick call. Signing up is free, so is pausing or cancelling your deliveries.
