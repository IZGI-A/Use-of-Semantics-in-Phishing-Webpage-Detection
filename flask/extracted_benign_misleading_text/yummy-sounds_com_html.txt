WE LOVE WHAT WE DO
Yummy Sounds is a family run company and we love our baby!
We have developed Yummy Sounds since 2015 for commercial marketing agency’s, motion designers & filmmaker. I am motion designer & composer myself and for my job I was looking for more music than I can create on my own. I spend hours & days on stock music pages and I got sick of listening through all the bad quality music.
I simply decided to watch out for the best composers in the world to create my own little library. So I asked all the composers in the team to create a library with their world-class music and they fully agreed, so we started Yummy Sounds together and we simply love it!
WORLD-CLASS & HANDPICKED
Our composer team is small, but they are truly world-class. We select carefully our composers and listen to every single track to make sure we only have the best available music on Yummy Sounds. We know your time is valuable and you will notice on Yummy Sounds you find a lot faster what you are actually looking for.
Our composers are working really close with us, and they are always ready to make adjustments on all tracks from the library, and we also create custom and personalized sound designs for you.
We are ready to handle this process for you fast, affordable and professional.
