Contessina London is a design brand based in London.
It is a fashion brand with exclusive design concepts, inclusive and conscious philosophy with the aspiration to make a positive impact on our society and environment.
Attention to details, high quality of materials, fine artisanship, functionality, sustainability and individuality are key elements in all of the collections.
We collaborate with small workshops internationally, wishing to bring closer to the market the value of remarkable worldwide traditional making techniques and exceptional artistic skills.
Through this site, we share with you our creative journey and invite you to a meaningful shopping experience!
