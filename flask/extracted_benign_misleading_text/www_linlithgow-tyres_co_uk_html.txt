Linlithgow Tyres & Exhausts are a family run local tyre specialist, we supply and fit for the public and trade. Our centre has been serving Linlithgow and the surrounding West Lothian area since 1987, offering personal service and local knowledge along with an excellent range of tyre brands to suit all pockets and vehicles.
We are a FALKEN and GENERAL Tyre Dealer, and between them, millions have been fitted by major manufacturers for new vehicles but still at a mid range price.
From our extensive knowledge and relationships with the major National suppliers you can be assured of the best possible price for all your tyres, exhaust and battery requirements. We can also supply tyres for specialist 4x4 and competition needs.
Our top quality tyre machines and fully trained fitters can safely cope with even the most exclusive alloy wheels.
As well as tyres we also offer wheel balancing, tracking, puncture repairs, batteries and exhausts.
You can use this website to book your tyres or pop in and see us if you’re not sure what you want and would like some advice. Whatever your needs please allow Linlithgow Tyres and Exhausts the opportunity to deliver value, quality and customer service. We are confident that you will be pleased that you did.
