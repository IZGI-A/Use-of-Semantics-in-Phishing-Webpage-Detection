Robert Staveley Aitchison
1Robert Staveley Aitchison was born on March 26, 1920 in Muendeniya, Ceylon. He was born to George Staveley Aitchison and Lilian Catherine (Rae) Aitchison , with sibling Nerina. He died on August 12, 1940 in near Witney, United Kingdom at 20 years of age. His cause of death is listed as: flying training accident. Robert was buried in Ifield, England.
Robert's education includes: Haileybury College.
