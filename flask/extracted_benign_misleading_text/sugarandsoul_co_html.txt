Pumpkin Cream Cheese Bread
By Rebecca Hubbell Leave a Comment
Pumpkin Cream Cheese Bread is a delicious fall quick bread recipe loaded with pumpkin flavor and swirled with caramel cream cheese filling. This is a sweet twist on my classic Pumpkin Bread recipe that the whole family will love just as much as Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies! If you can’t get enough pumpkin this season, this Cream Cheese Pumpkin Bread will definitely fill your craving. This super moist bread is made extra indulgent with a smooth cream cheese caramel layer that swirls throughout the loaf. Yep, you read that right: there’s caramel in the swirl!
