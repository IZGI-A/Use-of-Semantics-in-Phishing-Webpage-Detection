Shortlanesend Covid-19 Support Group
Welcome along to the Shortlanesend Covid 19 Support Group. This group has been established to act as a helpful central resource for people and businesses in the Shortlanesend area. Please use this to ask for and offer help, promote your services or just join in any conversations so you're not going to feel alone. The group will live and thrive from positive comments and help, so please be kind.
