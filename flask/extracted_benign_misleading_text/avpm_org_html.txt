The Association of Visual Packaging Manufacturers (AVPM) is a non-profit organization of packaging manufacturers, suppliers, educators, students and others that encourages networking and forming mutually beneficial relationships while aiding each other in raising and discussing issues facing the packaging industry.
AVPM also assists in connecting members to job opportunities while keeping members current on legal issues and the state of the industry as a whole.
AVPM also provides scholarships to students at accredited packaging schools.
