How Hong Kong protests try to be "like water"
"Be water" is a mantra of Hong Kong's pro-democracy protesters. As confrontations with police intensify, peaceful marches have faded from the global spotlight. But most protesters – resilient, flowing, nimble – still embody the "be water" slogan. Follow us into the ever-shifting streams of umbrellas, chants, and impromptu protest art.
