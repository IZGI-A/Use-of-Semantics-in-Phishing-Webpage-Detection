Banff & Lake Louise has a rich heritage as one of the world’s most awe-inspiring mountain destinations. With easy access to pristine wilderness, endless outdoor adventures, and all the amenities you need, in Banff National Park you feel truly immersed in nature.
There are events happening all year-round in Banff and Lake Louise. Be wowed and inspired by film screenings and talks from international writers and artists; celebrate the seasons with special events and performances; and dance under the stars at an outdoor concert. Have fun and seize the chance to experience something new with Banff and Lake Louise’s events offerings.
