CALIPSO is a web-based application that manages key aspects of academic and clinical education designed specifically and exclusively for speech-language pathology and audiology training programs.
It was created by a Communication Sciences and Disorders University Clinical Director and has demonstrated proven success.
Our application offers the unique feature of interactive and customized data dashboards that enable knowledge management between the clinical administrator, supervisor, and student.
CALIPSO is affordably priced for any sized institution.
We are pleased to announce that CALIPSO for SLP® is used by 70% of all Speech-Language Pathology programs and CALIPSO for Audiology® is used by 45% of all Audiology programs with a retention rate of 99%.
