We hope you enjoyed Giveaway Club! It is with the deepest regret, Giveaway Club team is announcing that after four years of helping people discover and download great software for free through direct partnership with developers, we will be shutting down on July, 26, 2019.
We are proud of the community that gathered around Giveaway Club. From now on, we will put our hearts and effort into expanding our core brand Auslogics and, in particular, its blog.
We will be happy to see you in our new enhanced blog to find out about special deals and information worth knowing.
