Mission
The mission of the KWES PTO is to promote and encourage communication between parents, teachers, administration and the community. In addition, the KWES PTO:
-
Inspires student achievement and enriches the learning environment for staff and students.
-
Supports activities throughout the school year such as family social events, teacher appreciation, special event assemblies, scholarships, and spirit wear in lieu of enlisting product sales.
-
Promotes transparency and builds partnerships and camaraderie between our families and our school.
