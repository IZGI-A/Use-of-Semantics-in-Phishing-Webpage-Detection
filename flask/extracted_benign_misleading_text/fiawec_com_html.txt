Welcome to endurance racing, welcome to our world
Season 8 ushers in a fresh new era for the FIA World Endurance Championship, with a cross-year 2019-2020 calendar format over ten months. This allows the WEC to place its jewel in the crown – the legendary 24 Hours of Le Mans – as the Super Finale to the season. Season 8 also welcomes back two new circuits [Bahrain and São Paulo], meaning teams and drivers will contest eight rounds across Asia, Europe, the Middle East, North America and South America – making the WEC a truly global championship. With up to 34 cars on track at the same time, the WEC is a sportscar extravaganza that offers action, overtakes, excitement and entertainment for fans worldwide.
