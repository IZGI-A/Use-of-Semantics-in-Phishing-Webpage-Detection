* Do you know what the SOA record is in DNS?
Suggested article from our blog: What is SOA (Start of Authority) record?
The SOA means the Start Of Authority. Every domain must have a Start of Authority record at the cutover point where the domain is delegated from its parent domain. The SOA record indicates which Domain Name Server (DNS) is the best source of information for the specified domain.
