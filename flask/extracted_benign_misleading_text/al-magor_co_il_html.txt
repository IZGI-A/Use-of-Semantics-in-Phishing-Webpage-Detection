AL-MAGOR Professional Tools
Al-Magor was founded 25 years ago and is engaged in the development and production of unique and original tools for punching holes and inserting connectors in irrigation pipes. These products are hand-crafted and meticulously designed to create precise holes for the installation of drippers, start connectors, and sprinklers into irrigation pipes. The tools developed by the company are made in cooperation with the largest irrigation pipes manufacturers like Netafim, Metzer, Rivulis, and others.
