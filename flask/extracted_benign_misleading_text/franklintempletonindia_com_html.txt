For first timers, mutual funds may sound complex, but in reality mutual funds are anything but intimidating. Mutual funds are in fact for the benefit of those who want to take the advantage of investing across equity, debt, gold, etc, but may not have the skill set, time or financial wherewithal to do so. This is exactly where a mutual fund comes in as it is a professionally managed collective investment vehicle that lets you invest in small amounts and reap big benefits, so long as you choose the right schemes in line with the goals you have set for yourself. Mutual funds also provide you multiple advantages of flexibility, liquidity, tax benefits and diversification of risk at a very small price. In this video we decode mutual funds for you.
