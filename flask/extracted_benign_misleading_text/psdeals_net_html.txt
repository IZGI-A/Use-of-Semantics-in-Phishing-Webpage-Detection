PS Deals - a unique PlayStation Games Price Tracker
PS Deals helps you track PlayStation games prices simply by subscribing to any game you'd like to buy. Let's see how it works:
1
Choose your PlayStation console and region in the top menu (on the main page - under the banner)
2
Use search or go to the 'Explore' section to find any game you'd like to buy cheaper
3
Press the 'Notify when price drops' button on the game page to receive free notification when the deal comes up (registration required)
That's it! Now sit back and relax - we will notify you by email when the price drops. Also check all currently available deals in 'Discounts' section and don't forget to download PS Deals iOS app!
