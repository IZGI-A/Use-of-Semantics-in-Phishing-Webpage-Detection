* Do you know what the FQDN is?
Suggested article from our blog: What is FQDN? What does FQDN do?
FQDN meaning:
FQDN or the Fully Qualified Domain Name is the fullest possible domain name of a host or a computer. It consists of three different parts.
FQDN example:
Let’s take a look at a popular website like www.wikipedia.org. In this example, “www” is the hostname, “wikipedia” is the domain name, and “.org” is the TLD (Top Level Domain). The FQDN shows us the exact location of an object inside the DNS hierarchy.
