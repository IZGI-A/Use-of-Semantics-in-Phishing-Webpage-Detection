About Me
Originally from Ann Arbor, Michigan, Gabrielle is currently a BFA Acting major at Oakland University. Having fallen in love with performing at a young age, she later discovered her love of stage and special effects makeup and has worked on several short films in the southeast Michigan area, where she quickly became known as the actor who came with her own fake blood.
When not performing or designing hair and makeup for shows, Gabrielle can be found hiking, baking pie, reading comics, throwing axes, or hanging out with her dog, Watson.
