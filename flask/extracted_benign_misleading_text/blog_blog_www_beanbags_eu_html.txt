* Do you know what the FQDN is?
Suggested article from our blog: What is FQDN? What does FQDN do?
FQDN definition:
The abbreviation means Fully Qualified Domain Name. It is the fullest, domain name of a computer or a host. It includes 3 parts.
FQDN example:
In the case of www.google.com, the .com is the Top Level Domain (TLD), the name of the domain is google, and the hostname is www.
With the FQDN, you will get the exact location of the object on the DNS hierarchy.
If you want to know how to perform a FQDN lookup, you can see examples here. Of course, you can do it, no matter if you are using Windows 10, macOS, or Linux.
