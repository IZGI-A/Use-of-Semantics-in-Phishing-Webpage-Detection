South Norfolk Community League
Established 2007
SNCL youth league cricket in Norfolk
SNCL summer league cricket
• The South Norfolk Community League provides competitive opportunities for young cricketers at U9, U11, U13, U15 & U17 age groups throughout the summer months.
• SNCL coverage, along with South Norfolk, includes clubs North and East of Norwich.
Winter zipleague
• zipleague is our winter indoor U14 competition.
• Starts in November at Notre Dame High School, Norwich.
• Six a side twelve over games lasting one hour.
• All equipment provided
