UK Mortgage Approvals at 6-Month Low: UK Finance
British banks approved 42,310 mortgages for new house purchase in September 2019, below a downwardly revised 42,527 mortgages in August, data from trade association UK Finance showed. Approvals of loans secured on dwellings for remortgaging increased to 32,649 from 32,225 in August, its highest level since November 2017; while those for other purposes declined to 9,079 from 9,124. Net mortgage lending slowed to GBP 2.333 billion in September, and net credit card lending rose to GBP 0.081 billion from GBP 0.059 billion. Still, the annual growth of credit card lending by major banks grew to 3.0 percent.
