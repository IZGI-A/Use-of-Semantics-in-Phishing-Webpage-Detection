Located in the mountains and hidden by smog is the city of scientists and entrepreneurs, of the hopefuls and the runaways. Welcome to Golden City. Where the narrow streets weave into each other like clockwork and each layer holds its own secrets.
In this world, superpowers exist. From the dawn of time, to an age when superheroes flaunted their abilities in spandex, to a day when the earth is on its last legs, desolate due to our unnatural abilities. Explore stories told across time as humanity struggles with gifts that they may never understand.
