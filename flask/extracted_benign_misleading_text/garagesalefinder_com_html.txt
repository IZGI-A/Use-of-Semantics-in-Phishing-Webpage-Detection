GarageSaleFinder.com is free tool to help you find garage sales and yard sales easier than ever before. With our free garage sale map, you not only can see at-a-glance where the garage sale hot spots are, you can print turn-by-turn directions to the garage sales of your choice. And, for the technically inclined, you can download the list of garage sales to your personal GPS device and go "high tech." (For more about GPS downloads, visit the GPS section of our blog.)
