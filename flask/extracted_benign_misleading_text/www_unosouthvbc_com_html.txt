We are the "One Club" for boys and girls. We are an extension of our home club UNO VBC in Joliet, IL www.unovbc.com. We are affiliated with USA Volleyball. We offer skill development and a high level of competition. Our athletes that choose to travel, we compete in the Windy City Power League (WCPL) in the Chicagoland Area.
We are hosted by Game Time Gym II, in Bloomington, IL.
