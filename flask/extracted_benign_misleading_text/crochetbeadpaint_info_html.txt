Welcome to the online pattern editor for crocheted bead ropes.
Here you can create and share your own patterns or choose one of many
thousands ready ones, shared by our designers, for editing, reusing or
just getting inspired by.
Use lots of useful little features like beads
calculator of 3D-Diagram builder.
Signing in with your user account is absolutely crucial, but also
absolutely free of charge.
Have fun!
